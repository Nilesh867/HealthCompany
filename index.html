To enroll in a plan in 2024, you must first be enrolled in both Medicare Part A and Part B. You will still need to pay your Part B premium each month as well. Plans are required to offer as good of coverage as both Parts A and B. Above what is covered in Medicare Part A and B, additional plan benefits from insurance companies offering plans will vary by company and area.
